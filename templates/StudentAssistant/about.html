    Through our website we want to create an interface between the Students of NSU and the Authority in a friendly manner in order to help the students with poor grades. Our vision is to provide a bridge between NSU Students and NSU Authority which would at least provide better grades to the students for their better future.

  • The objective of this project is to create a rule based Expert System(ES) for the academic advising of the students of the Electrical & Computer Engineering Department at North South University.
  • This system helps the students in course selection for each academic semester.
  • This system provide proper guidelines for the students
